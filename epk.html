In the spirit of the Buffalo music scene, lets take a moment to share. Incase you haven't heard them, picture prog, disco, soul, metal. These guys put down delicious composed instrumental grooves and rocking riffs in a variety of time signatures and colors. Decorated with cascading waterfalls of harmonic modal emotion by guitar player Mike Cassidy, as well as crisp arpeggios and literal laser beams coming from guitar player Max Davis, bassist Tom Varco and drummer Dan Gagliardi hold down a textured landscape of time, space, and rhythm. Just coming off of their fall tour, you can catch @tortoiseforest at their monthly residency at Tudor lounge, or on the interwebs (Spotify, etc.) by searching for their self titled album.
